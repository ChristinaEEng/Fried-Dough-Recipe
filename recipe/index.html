Grammy Lu Lu's Fried Dough

Do you ever have to make your grandchildren food but don't want to put in that much effort? Grammy Lu Lu has the recipe for you!

Just follow these simple steps and you'll be a great grandmother.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Oil

Time

Prep Time
20 minutes

Directions

  • Drive to the store and pick up some pizza dough
  • Come back home
  • Put some oil in a pan
  • Heat up the pan on medium heat
  • Feel the air above the oil to see if its hot
  • Rip off little chunks of dough off and place them in the oil

***** DO NOT SPLASH THE HOT OIL EVERYWHERE AND BURN YOUR GRANDCHILDREN *****

  • When the dough balls get light brown, flip them with a pair of tongs
  • Place dough on a plate lined with paper towels
  • Serve with a bowl of confectionary sugar and/or cinnamon sugar

This recipe may seem extremely simple... and it is... But I'll be damned if you arent the best grandmother in the world.
